HOW TO PLAY

						Object:
						You are the green player, the object of this game is to kill
						as much enemies as possible.

						Gameplay:
						U can move the player with the W,A,S,D keys. When u click on 
						the left mouse button, the player starts shooting. After u
						finished the game, u can enter a username. Select with the
						arrowkeys youre username. When u have selected a username,
						then press Enter and it will save the score.
